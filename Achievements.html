International Achievements


Represented USA in the 2011 Pan American Games hosted in Jamaica and was a semi-finalist in the 2009 Pan American Games hosted in Puerto Rico. Also Qualified for the BWF 2011 World Junior Championships hosted in Taiwan.

National Achievements


Top Junior Nationals results include 2nd place in under 19 men's doubles and 3rd place in under 22 men's doubles. Was also one of 4 players in the US to recieve the All American Award, given to high school students who exceed highly in badminton and in school.

Collegiate Achievements


Undefeated in the 2014 Boston Open Collegiate Division. Placed 1st in men's doubles and 1st in mixed doubles. Also coached and competed for Amherst College in the 2014 New England Junior Championships placing 5th in division 1.
