Piece Movement


Simply pick up or click a piece and the colorful icons will light up. Marked spaces on the board indicate legal moves. Blue circles represent free spaces and red squares indicate occupied spaces. When a piece is clicked, a blue tile underneath it glows to show which piece is currently selected. If a player tries to move the piece to an unmarked position the indicators disapear.

Restricted Indicators


The move markers don't just show every position a piece can go to. Moves that put the user in check are eliminated, allowing quicker decision making.

Easy Check Signal


When in check, a transparent red overlay covers the board. It stays on the board until the player moves out of check. While in check, selected pieces only show moves that get the king out of check.

Follows All Strict Chess Rules


Includes commonly known rules such as pawn promotion and stalemate as well as less comonly known ones such as en passant and castling. This version of chess follows all rules entirely.

Pawn Promotion


Come to the end of the board with a pawn and don't just simply recieve a queen. A popup allows the user to pick exactly which piece it wants to be rewarded with.

Checkmate!


When the game comes to an end and someone gets checkmate, a darker red overlay covers the board. A message pops up displaying the winner. When the user clicks "Ok" the game is reset.
