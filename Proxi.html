Anonymous Users


Fire up Proxi and up to 5 annonymous users will flood into your screen. All users can post statuses to share what they feel like talking about with other users such as common interests.

Anonymous Chatting


Feeling lonely or see a status that piques your interest? Click on a user and begin having a care free conversation with them without feeling any social presures. Without an account you have nothing to worry about if people will judge you for your appearance or history. You can be whoever you want to be without any social restrictions.

Clean Slate Every Time


Not feeling a currect conversation? You can easily exit the conversation and shake the device to explore more friends. Flick friends off the screen to remove them and new local users will again populate your screen.
