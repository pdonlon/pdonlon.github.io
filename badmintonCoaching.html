Amherst College


Became the head coach of the Amherst Badminton Club in the fall of 2012, since then the badminton club has been completely changed. The club was transformed from a social club with no structure or guidence to a collegiate competitor amoung the East Coast. With structured practices and formal instruction, players learn proper technique, strategy, attitude, and leadership on court. Since becoming head coach the club also became apart of the Intercollegiate Badminton Association and has began challanging rival colleges such as Williams College, Yale, and MIT. The club has also traveled to team collegiate tournaments in both Boston and New Jersey.

Marblehead Youth Badminton


Assistant Badminton Coach of Marblehead Youth Badminton from 2010 through 2011. Independently ran and organized groups of different skill levels ranging from beginner to international and ages ranging from 7 to 18. Traveled with the team to coach regional and national tournaments. Taught students to have a discipline to become top competitors in the United States while still keeping the passion and love for the sport.
