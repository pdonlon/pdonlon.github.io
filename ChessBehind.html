Chess uses different types of pieces that all extend one piece class. This piece has attributes that all the pieces share such as movement arrays. Each piece has an array of moves which are determined by that pieces rules, where other pieces are in the board, and if it will cause the king to become in check. All moves are tested to see if they get the king into check, those moves that get him into check are removed. When moving a piece, the colors lighting is just the valid move array.
