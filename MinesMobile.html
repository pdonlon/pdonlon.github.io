Detailed Animations


Simply tap anywhere on the board and watch the beautiful tile animation flood through the opened tiles. Opened tiles display the number of bombs touching them in a subtle white font over uniquely colored tiles. Each second that ticks by in the game is reflected by the accurate timer's minute hand, taking exaclty 60 seconds to fully make its way around the clock.

Easy Yet Advanced Controls


Switch between 2 modes, open and flag mode, by tapping the mine or flag logo in the bottom bar. While in open mode tap any tile to open it or long press to flag it. In flag mode tap any tile to flag it or long press to open it. A convenient vibration is fired when the long press is triggered so you don't have to pick up your finger to check. In any mode, when the amount of flags touching a tile is the same as its number displayed simply touch the tile and it will open the remaining tiles surrounding it.

Never Miss a Beat


Never lose your game progress ever again! Whenever the game is interupted by calls, alarms, or just leaving the application, the game will be paused and saved until returning. Even if the app is killed in the background, Mines will resume from where you left!

Multiplayer Mode


A Mines Mobile exclusive, multiplayer mode! Pick to host or join a game and get ready to race your friends against the clock. The host gives his opponent a randomly generated code which gives both opponents the same playing field. This game mode does not require blue-tooth since it utilizes seed generation.

Personal Content Saved


Mines Mobile now saves high scores. With the addition of high scores, users are challenged to beat their records keeping the game fun game after game! Mines mobile also saves setting preferences for the vibration and animation toggle to allow the user a more customized game experience.

More Control Over the Board


Can't seem to see the whole board or need to zoom in for more accuracy? Use the 2 magnifying icons in the bottom right of the game bar to change the view. Tap the minus magnifying glass to zoom out and the plus magnifying glass to zoom in. There is no limit to how much you can zoon in or out for all screen sizes and preferences.

No Better Feeling


Aside from not being startled by the vibrations of each bomb ticking off from losing, nothing is more rewarding then seeing the navigation bar icons glow green, having your score appear, and if you're lucky breaking a new high score. Now can you beat it even faster?
